Caesar Salad Recipe

A nice and light appetizer that will not completely fill you up but leave with you something that keep you satisfied when you don't feel like eatting a complete meal or while you wait for one. The best part about this recipe is that the ingredients that it uses are easily accessible and not complicated to cook, so you won't have to spend long in the kitchen if you short on time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large Romaine Lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Crisp croutons
  • Caesar Dressing of your choice
  • 1 Large Bowl
Steps:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, add your prepared romaine lettuce, top with croutons and parmesan.
  2. Just before serving, drizzle with your dressing, and toss gently to coat the lettuce and croutons.
